Process

From roll to report in three passes.

Upload

Feed a swatch photo, or connect a line-scan camera to inspect fabric continuously as it comes off the loom.

02

Inspect

A convolutional pass runs over every thread line, comparing the weave against a clean reference pattern.

03

Defect Coverage

Six defect classes, one pass.

Trained on mill-floor imagery across cotton, poly-blend and denim weaves.

Holes & tears

Punctures and torn threads from tension faults or worn needles.

avg. 97% confidence

Stains & oil marks

Roller oil, dye bleed and moisture discoloration.

avg. 95% confidence

Slubs

Irregular thick spots in the yarn that break the weave's evenness.

avg. 94% confidence

Misweave & broken pick

Skipped or snapped weft threads that leave a visible gap.

avg. 91% confidence

Color shading

Batch-to-batch dye variation visible across roll width.

avg. 89% confidence

Foreign yarn

Mismatched fiber or color pulled into the weave by accident.

avg. 93% confidence
